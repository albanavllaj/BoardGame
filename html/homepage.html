How to Play 'Don't Get Mad

Objective:

Welcome to the game of "Don't Get Mad"! This fun and competitive game is all about luck, and staying cool under pressure. The goal is to be the first player to get to the last tile while trying to sabotage your opponent.

Gameplay:

  • The game will be only a 2 Player game.
  • Players take turns.
  • You start the game only when you roll a 6.
  • The dice rolls up to 6.
  • If you roll a 6, you get an extra turn.
  • If you roll a 6 you roll again up to three times and the number of tiles you are going to move adds up
  • If you land on an opponent's tile, you "bump" them back to their starting area.
  • If your game piece lands on a ladder, you get to climb it and advance to the higher-numbered cell at the top of the ladder. Ladders are shortcuts in the game.
  • If your game piece lands on a snake, you must slide down it and move to the lower-numbered cell at the tail of the snake. Snakes are obstacles that set you back in the game.
